Doggy Dates

Because everyone deserves love

He may be your best friend, but doesn't he deserve a date of his own?

Doggy Dates is a new and exciting app that allows you to find dogs in your local area for your doggy to go on date-walks with.

Whether it's that cool Jack Russel owned by the cute hipster with the beard, or the friendly Border Collie owned by the mysterious lady who lives down the road Doggy Dates can help you connect.


And who knows? You could even find a mate of your own.
